Wenhao Ding received his bachelar degree in Department of Electronic Engineering, School of Information Science and Technology, Tsinghua University in Jul, 2018. He is now working at Aurora Lab at Tsinghua University as an engineer.

I am looking for a PhD. position of 2019 Fall. If you are interested in my research, please feel free to contact me.

He is interested in both theoretical and applicational areas of machine learning. He mainly focuses on deep generative models and representation learning, especially Generative Adversarial Networks and Variational Auto-Encoder. He also follows multiple tracks of robotics application, such as robot perception, multi-agent collaboration and autonomous driving.

Besides doing research about high-level application, he aslo make some projects of low-level hardware and architecture design, which are also significant for the whole robot system.

Projects

Air Hockey Robot

Jun 13, 2015

Air hockey is a popular table game, and this robot can play this game with human beings. The bird-view camera captures the position of the ball and the comuputer controls the mechanical arm with a loop feedback.


Coorperation Moble Robot Platform

Jul 18, 2016

This is a platform of coorperative cars, which are equipped with UWB to communicate with each other. With the distance information, cars are able to dynamically form a pre-defined formation. These cars are self-made and controlled by STM32 micro-chip.


Tsinghua University Electronic Design Competition

Aug 18, 2015

This is an annual competition in Tsinghua university, aiming to improve students' innovation and skills. We, the organizer of the competition, build the hardware and software platform for contestants.


Micro Drone

Jul 15, 2017

We built a micro drone based on a piece of PCB and it can be controlled by an APP on Android phone. The size of this drone is about 9cm*9cm.


BLE Localization System

Jul 18, 2017

We developd a set of device that can use Received Signal Strength Indicator (RSSI) of Bluetooth signal to measure the distance between source device and the receiver. With reasonable layout, this device can work as a rough positioning system.


3D reconstruction

Jul 3, 2017

This project utilizes ASUS Xtion and Realsense to reconstruct rooms and objects and output a raw pointcloud. The algorithm is a basic SLAM method consisting of a frond-end of ORB and a back-end of G2O. Code is avaliable HERE.
